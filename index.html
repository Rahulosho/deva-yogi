About Deva

I am Deva — a healer and guide rooted in the path of Feel – Reveal – Heal. My work blends movement, breath, sound, and meaningful conversation to help people explore their inner world and reconnect with their authentic self.

My journey has been shaped not by certificates, but by real-life experiences — learning from teachers across India, Southeast Asia, and Europe, and by deeply listening to the wisdom of the body.

My approach integrates traditional and intuitive frameworks including the Five Koshas, Elements, Chakras, and my own creation — the Spanda Nervous System — guiding others toward greater clarity and wholeness.

In addition to my practice, I also serve as a holistic psychotherapist and educator at the Inner Process Academy of Somatic Psychotherapists. My approach weaves together a deep understanding of neuroscience, trauma healing, and somatic techniques, supporting others through real transformation.

Feel – Reveal – Heal

A New Way to Understand Sound Healing, Life & Transformation

FEEL

To feel is the beginning of healing. But many of us stop here—because feeling is not always easy. We live in a world that teaches us to be busy, to numb, to avoid pain, to stay “strong.” But healing does not start in strength. It starts in sensitivity.

In sound healing, the first step is to feel: your stress, tension, confusion, anxiety, fatigue. But also—your breath, heartbeat, and body in the present moment.

With sound—bowls, voice, breath—we create space for your nervous system to listen again.
Feel means: “I meet myself.”

REVEAL

After feeling comes truth. And with truth comes release. When you truly feel what’s inside—your fear, pain, patterns—truth rises.

Reveal is not about showing pain to others. It’s about being honest with yourself.

Sound helps this revelation. Your breath may shift, emotions may surface, and your body releases.
Reveal means: “I face my truth.”

HEAL

Healing happens not from outside, but because you are now ready and true. It’s not about becoming someone new, but returning to who you really are.

The body relaxes deeply, breath opens, the brain forms new patterns, and energy flows.
Heal means: “I return to life.”

The 5 Koshas & Elements

In sound healing, we work through the five Koshas, or layers of being:

Annamaya Kosha – Earth: Physical body. Feeling grounded and safe.
Pranamaya Kosha – Water: Energy body. Emotion, breath, life force.
Manomaya Kosha – Fire: Mind and emotion. Burn illusions, gain clarity.
Vijnanamaya Kosha – Air: Intuition. Deep inner guidance.
Anandamaya Kosha – Space: Bliss. Presence. Union with all.

Sound heals across these layers—aligning body, mind, spirit, and soul.

Neocortex (Insight)
