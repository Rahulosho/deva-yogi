About Deva

I am Deva — a healer and guide rooted in the path of Feel – Reveal – Heal. My work blends movement, breath, sound, and meaningful conversation to help people explore their inner world and reconnect with their authentic self.

My journey has been shaped not by certificates, but by real-life experiences — learning from teachers across India, Southeast Asia, and Europe, and by deeply listening to the wisdom of the body.

My approach integrates traditional and intuitive frameworks including the Five Koshas, Elements, Chakras, and my own creation — the Spanda Nervous System — guiding others toward greater clarity and wholeness.

In addition to my practice, I also serve as a holistic psychotherapist and educator at the Inner Process Academy of Somatic Psychotherapists. My approach weaves together a deep understanding of neuroscience, trauma healing, and somatic techniques, supporting others through real transformation.

The Deva Philosophy

Feel – Reveal – Heal

More than a method — it's a way of being.

Feel – Reveal – Heal is not just a healing method. It is a profound philosophy of being human — a path that touches every layer of your life: Physical, Mental, Emotional, Psychological, Spiritual, and Universal.

This is not only about sound. This is about you. It connects to your subtle body and spiritual unfolding, working through the nervous system, energy field, chakras, koshas, tattvas, and nadis. But more than anything, it works with your truth.

FEEL

To feel is to return home to your body. Before healing begins, you must land in your breath, bones, and sensations. Feeling is the beginning of honesty — with yourself and your pain.

REVEAL

What has been hidden starts to rise — the old voices, ancient grief, frozen fire. Through sound, your subconscious finds a voice. This is where the truth reveals itself — not through thought, but through vibration.

HEAL

Healing is the result, not the beginning. It happens when your system remembers its original rhythm — when all of you is seen, felt, and loved back into harmony.

This is Deva’s philosophy. A reflection of humanity itself.

Chakras Through Feel – Reveal – Heal

1. Root Chakra – Muladhara

Feel: Where do I feel unsafe?

Reveal: I uncover the fears, survival stress, and inherited patterns.

Heal: I come back to ground. I teach others how to use sound to create safety and stability.

Spanda: The first pulse of life—dense but alive, like the heartbeat of the earth.

2. Sacral Chakra – Svadhisthana

Feel: Where do I suppress my pleasure, desire, or emotions?

Reveal: I see my guilt, shame, or emotional blocks.

Heal: I reconnect to fluidity, creativity, sensuality.

Spanda: The wave of emotion—the dance of water inside the body.

3. Solar Plexus – Manipura

Feel: Where is my power blocked?

Reveal: I face my anger, lack of confidence, or control.

Heal: I rise with clarity and strength.

Spanda: The fire that transforms—energy rising upward.

4. Heart Chakra – Anahata

Feel: Where do I close my heart?

Reveal: I touch grief, heartbreak, and isolation.

Heal: I soften and expand. I teach the sound of compassion.

Spanda: The silent vibration—pure pulse of love.

5. Throat Chakra – Vishuddha

Feel: Where is my voice held back?

Reveal: I meet fear of judgment and silence.

Heal: I express truth and sing freely.

Spanda: Sound becoming word—the breath of truth.

6. Third Eye – Ajna

Feel: Where is my perception unclear?

Reveal: I see illusions and mental clutter.

Heal: I trust my inner vision.

Spanda: Vibration of insight—pulse of knowing.

7. Crown Chakra – Sahasrara

Feel: Where do I feel disconnected from life?

Reveal: I face my spiritual wounds and existential doubt.

Heal: I dissolve separation through sound and silence.

Spanda: The final echo—expanding into the infinite.
