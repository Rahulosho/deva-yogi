About Deva

I am Deva — a healer and guide rooted in the path of Feel – Reveal – Heal. My work blends movement, breath, sound, and deep conversation to support people in meeting their pain, patterns, and power.

I’ve learned not through certificates, but through life — guided by teachers across India, Southeast Asia, and Europe, and by listening deeply to the body. My approach weaves together the Five Koshas, Elements, Chakras, and my own creation, the Spanda Nervous System, to help others remember who they are.
